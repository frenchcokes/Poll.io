About
Welcome to Poll.io! This is a free multiplayer brower game where you and your friends write answers to prompts and vote on your favorite responses! Players are scored based on how many votes they get, and the player with the most points at the end of the game wins! Good luck and enjoy!
Info on Packs
Default: General and standard prompts.
Default2: More unstable prompts than default.
Friends: Prompts that will only be fun if you know the people you're playing with.
How to play
  1. Enter a name and join a room with your friends!
  2. Choose some prompts packs!
  3. Write an answer to the prompt!
  4. Vote on your favorite answers!
  5. Repeat for multiple rounds!
  6. Win by having the most points!
Feedback
Have any prompts or suggestions? Let us know! If your prompt is good and you write a non-offensive name, I'll add it and credit you! You can also email us.
